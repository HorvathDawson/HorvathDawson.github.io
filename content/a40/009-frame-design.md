The frame is being designed from scratch in CAD. It's a ladder frame, two main rails with crossmembers, built around all the suspension and drivetrain mounting points.

Major dimensions are driven by:
- Body mount locations (measured off the original frame)
- Front suspension pickup points (from the narrowing analysis)
- Four-link rear mounting brackets
- Motor and transmission mounting
- Steering rack location
- Firewall clearances for the SR20DET

Will be updated with CAD screenshots and key dimensions as the design progresses.
