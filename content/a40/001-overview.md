This is just a log for myself as I work through putting this car together. The A40 is a 1950 Austin Devon, body-on-frame, going onto a completely custom ladder frame with narrowed MX-5 front suspension, a triangulated four-bar rear with a narrowed Ford 8", an SR20DET, and Miata disc brakes all around on a unified 4x100 bolt pattern.

Posts are organized by topic. Some of this is research, some is geometry work, some is fabrication. Updated as I go.
