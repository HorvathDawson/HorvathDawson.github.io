The stock A40 steering is unassisted and the column is a solid shaft pointed straight at your chest. Both of those need to change.

Plan is power assisted rack and pinion (likely electric power steering to keep it simple, no hydraulic lines to route) and a collapsible steering column. The collapsible column is non-negotiable from a safety standpoint.

More details once the front suspension geometry is finalized since the steering rack location depends on that.
