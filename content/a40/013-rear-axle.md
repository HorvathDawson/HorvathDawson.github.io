The rear axle is a narrowed Ford 8-inch. Picked it up with the second A40. It's one of the most common solid axles in hot rod builds, tons of aftermarket support. Ring and pinion ratios, limited-slip diffs, custom axle shafts in any length, all off the shelf.

It's being redrilled to 4x100 to match the Miata hubs and brakes up front. One bolt pattern, one set of wheels, all four corners.

Driveshaft will be custom length to connect the SR20DET's transmission output to the Ford 8". Will need to be balanced and built to the right specs once the motor and axle are in their final positions in the frame.

More details once the frame design is further along and the motor/trans position is locked in.
